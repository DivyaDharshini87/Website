IGEN SDG AWARDS 2022

Press Release of IGEN SDG ACTION AWARDS G01 The Institution of Green Engineers (IGEN) #theigen debut IGEN SDG ACTION AWARDS G01 witnessed on 30th September 2022, hosted and presented at KIT- KalaignarKarunanidhi Institute of technology, Coimbatore. The successful green action participants of #igensdgprojects will receive #igensdgactionawards. The Coimbatore region 120 winners of #igenenergy99challenge V1.0 Project received IGEN SDG ACTION AWARDS G01 with IGEN Carbon Credit Certificate.

The Award function was presided by the founder chairman of KIT KalaignarKarunanidhi Institute of Technology, Pongalur Thiru. N.Palanisamy and he stressed the importance of CO2 emission and the energy conservation project. He had presented #igensdgactionawards to the successful achievers. The Vice-Chairperson of KIT, Mrs. Indu Murugesan felicitate the winners and presented winners certification. Dr.M.Saravana Kumar (Dean- Anna University regional campus Coimbatore), Dr.L.Ashok Kumar (Chairman of IAEMP, Coimbatore chapter, Professor PSG College of Technology) and Dr Mohanasundaram (IGEN Director)presented, IGEN Carbon Credit Certificate to the contributors of #igenenergy99challenge. Dr.L.Ramesh IGEN President, congratulate all the winners of #igenenergy99challenge and proposed the concept of IGEN WALL OF FAME.
IGEN WALL OF FAME is a wall containing plaques commemorating notable individuals. This wall dedicated to the honouring of people who distinguished themselves in IGEN SDG Projects. The IGEN WALL OF FAME was released by Vice-Chairperson of KIT, Mrs. Indu Murugesan and first copy of FAME presented to IGEN President. In this event, Principal Dr.N. Mohandass Gandhi, Vice Principal Dr. M. Ramesh, Dean-Student Affairs Dr. S. Suresh, Dean Research Dr.K. Ramasamy,Head EEE Dr.R. Mythili, KIT- ENSAV club and E99C coordinators Mr.R.Thamizharasan ,Mr.T.Barath Kumar and faculty members of KIT and other various institution were present in the Ceremony.

The Coimbatore region Award winners are from KIT-Kalaignarkarunanidhi Institute of Technology, CSI College of Engineering, K. Ramakrishnan College of Engineering, KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology, Vivekanandha College of Technology for Women and JCT college of Engineering.


The Award ceremony was effectively planned and executed under the leadership of Dr S. Gomathi and Er D. Taejas with the team members Amb. Dr. S. Madan kumar, Dr. S. Suresh, Mr. R. Thamizharasan, Ms. Kakoli Malakar, Ms. P. Divya Dharshini, Mr. Kalilullah Ibrahim, Mr. S. Sanjay, Mr. T. Logesh and Mr. S. Aakash. We will meet you all at IGEN SDG Action Awards GO2.
